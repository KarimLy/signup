Overview on filling out a Legal Shield membership application

When you first get started it can be kind of tricky. If you subscribe to e-service and have a website where you can enroll members it's a lot easier and faster online. If you do not have e-service you'll have to fill out the Legal Shield membership applications manually. A few tips on this. The left hand side of the membership application is pretty straight forward. Just basic info. For the top right hand side you are going to want to check the first box, "Pre-Paid Legal Services. Inc." Under that chose whether they want the Standard Family Plan ($17.00/mo.).

If the future member want Identity Theft protection as well make sure to check that box. With the Legal Plan the basic Identity Theft plan adds an extra $9.95/mo to the total cost of the membership. For Associates Use Only, just fill out your Associate information and sign. Below that, make sure the member puts the City and State they are in as well as Dependents they may have and that they sign as well. Make sure to add the one-time enrollment fee of $10.00 and the total in the final box. The member will have to choose a payment option. Either Bank Draft or Debit/Credit Card. Most people do Debit/Credit card. Bank Draft is better because you don't have to worry about their credit card expiring.

Make sure the member signs in the payment area. After that, just mail in the application to the proper address. If you are not sure what address to send the application to make sure to contact me and I will give you the correct address.
